Key parallels 
Surveillance and Privacy: Technologies like CCTV networks and AI monitoring mirror the "telescreens" and Big Brother concepts from George Orwell’s 1984, eroding privacy and enabling state control. 
Ideological Control: The manipulation of truth through disinformation and propaganda reflects Orwell’s "Newspeak" and Huxley’s "Brave New World," where citizens are pacified by pleasure or confused by conflicting narratives. 
Social Stratification: Modern economic disparities echo the rigid class systems of dystopian narratives, where a privileged elite holds disproportionate wealth and power over marginalized groups. 
Themes of Rebellion: Both fictional and real-world societies exhibit themes of rebellion against perceived injustices, highlighting the ongoing struggle for individual rights and democratic values.
